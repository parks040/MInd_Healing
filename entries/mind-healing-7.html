The Beauty of Simple Things: My Journey to Happiness

Experiencing the beauty of each leaf's colorful reflections in the sunlight, following the strong wings of a white butterfly that never stops moving forward, watching ants carrying heavy pieces of candy on their backs even on a hot day when the world is wrapped tightly with threads so there's no room to breathe, hearing a single word of appreciation for my hard work after finishing my daily routine, sitting in a cafe and listening to people's conversations while enjoying a cool glass of peach iced tea with ice cubes floating in it, appreciating the clear colors amidst the blur of a long scene outside the window of a moving bus, feeling the warmth of a home filled with family laughter - all of these things make me feel at ease. They make me smile. They make me live passionately. They make me breathe. These are the reasons I live.
